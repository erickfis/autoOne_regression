Description¶

AutoOne is a company which buys and sells used cars in more than 30 countries across Europe.

The heart of their business is to chose the prices for buying and selling cars the best they can.

They have a lot of information on each car and they want to be able to make data driven decisions when deciding what are the best prices.

Therefore, for this challenge we will use the data available to build a statistical model for predicting prices for a given car.

In this section, we will cover the data cleaning process.
